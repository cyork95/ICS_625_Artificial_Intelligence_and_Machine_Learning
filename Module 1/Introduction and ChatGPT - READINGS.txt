What major corporate departments are involved in the implementation of AI?
Pretty much all the corporate departments are in some way involved. Front-line workers, front line supervisers, programmers,
and management

What level of management support is required to implement AI?
Full management support is required.

What is the impact of AI on workers?
Workers are having to change how they do their job to have AI enhance what they do. This may mean retraining and upskilling.
However, this is causing issues with shrinking entry-level jobs.
